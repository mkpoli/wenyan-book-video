“What is this ‘A’?
Why not call it ‘B’ instead?”

He replied:
“All are acceptable. ‘A’ is merely a name.
A name —
is the guest of a reality —
and what is that reality?
Whatever one pleases to assign to it.

If you wish it to be three, let it be three;
if you wish it to be five, let it be five.
This is called a variable.”
