“Yáo” (booleans) denotes
Yin and Yang.
“Yin and Yang” —
rightness and wrongness.
Being and non‑being.
Approval and rejection.
All things which possess two extremes
are of this sort.
“Lists” (arrays) denotes.
It is that by which one arrays the several things of a like kind.
For example, the prayer‑beads of the followers of the Buddha.
Or the long‑snake formation of the military arts.
“Things” (objects) denotes.
It is what portrays the manifold aspects of a single entity.
Compare it to a person's family name and personal name.
Those are words.
