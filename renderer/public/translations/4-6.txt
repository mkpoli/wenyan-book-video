Some may ask,
“Is the final word “jiaeq” (也),
a grammatical particle?
Can it be omitted?”
It is answered, “
No, it is not permissible.
It is used to distinguish the shades of meaning.
That marks the end of a sentence.
Indeed, the theory of programming may have extremes of complexity.
Some things are nested within others.
Some things interlock.
Without this, one cannot determine.
The two graphs un un (云云, ‘and so on’) may also suffice.”
