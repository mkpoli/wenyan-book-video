The Ten Philosophers of Confucius:
Yan Hui (Ziyuan, 子淵),
Min Sun (Ziqian, 子騫),
Ran Boniu (Ran Geng, 冉耕),
Zhong Gong (Ran Yong, 冉雍),
Zai Yu (Ziwo, 子我),
Zi Gong (Duanmu Ci, 端木賜),
Ran Qiu (Ziyou, 子有),
Zilu (Zhong You, 仲由),
Ziyou (Yan Yan, 言偃),
Zixia (Bu Shang, 卜商).
Together they are called the Former Wise Ones,
and their bearing is diverse.
Now within these four questions.
One may know who they are.
Their method is thus.
