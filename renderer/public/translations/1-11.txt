He devised a plan to make programming in the Chinese tongue,
and named his language “Wenyan” — Literary Speech.
Is it not fitting, then, that this book employs it?

In ancient times, all primers began with what was easiest to learn —
such was The Three-Character Classic.
Let us likewise begin from what is simplest.

The most basic example of programming is called “Ask Heaven and Earth if They Are Well.”
Its code is written as follows:

I have a number,
named three,
and I call it A.
