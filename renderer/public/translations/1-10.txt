So too does its usage depend on the task.

“Error reporting” means this:
whether by human mistake
or material limitation,
the machine cannot fulfill what the user intends —
thus it gives a message to inform the person of the cause.

In the world today, there are not fewer than a hundred programming languages,
each contending to show its excellence for a time.
Most, however, are creations of Westerners
and thus follow the patterns of Western speech.

Now there is Scholar Huang of Shanghai,
who has studied many texts in depth
and, imitating the elegant diction of the ancients,
has composed anew in refined classical style.
