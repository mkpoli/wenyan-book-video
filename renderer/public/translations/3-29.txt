I have four numbers.
Each of them are, three hundred forty
thirty-two,
two hundred and sixteen,
and four hundred and seventeen.
They are named, respectively, “Head,” “Arm,” “Foot,” and “Tail.”
Divide “Arm” by two.
Name it “Xing Tian.”
Multiply “Xing Tian” by two.
Subtract it from the “Foot.”
Divide it by four.
Name it “Sum of Hydra and Fox.”
