Moreover, all things are in constant change —
as once was Guangling in ancient days,
or the desolate city sung of by Bao Zhao.

Now, if we alter “A” to be four, it is thus:
what was once “A” —
is now four.

To write it out means:
that is the result obtained when the machine runs.
The machine itself knows it,
but man has no means to see it directly.
Therefore, we can use this command to make it display the outcome to us.
