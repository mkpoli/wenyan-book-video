Example two.
Ancient Lerna has a monster with nine heads, four feet, and one tail,
named the Hydra.
The Classic of Mountains and Seas records that in the Southern Mountains there is a fox with nine tails.
Xing Tian has no head.
Sun Shu-ao once saw a two-headed snake.
Now the King of Chu is hunting;
he has captured four kind of monsters, each some.
All were brought into the cage.
Above are three hundred and forty heads.
Thirty-two arms.
Below are two hundred sixteen feet.
Four hundred seventeen tails.
