Once it has been programmed,
the machine suddenly becomes clear and bright,
as though the clouds were parted and the sun revealed —
thus we ask Heaven and Earth if they are well.

Someone asked:
“Why ask three times?
Are Heaven and Earth deaf?
Haven't you heard of Qin Mi's debate?”

He replied:
“No —
it is to demonstrate the use of repetition, or looping.

‘Looping’ —
means to perform one action several times,
like the spokes of a wheel turning in unison.
