He replied:
“They serve only to aid the reader’s eyes;
their absence does not harm the meaning.
Thus, the earlier example may also be written in this way:

I have a number named three call it A repeat A times I have a phrase ‘Ask Heaven and Earth if They Are Well’ write it out and so on.

The example of ‘Ask Heaven and Earth if They Are Well’
should, by this, now be roughly understood.”
