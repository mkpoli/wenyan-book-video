As the sun and moon take turns in the sky, so too may loops repeat in their order.

Someone asked:
“If I wish to run it five times, may I?
If a thousand times, may I?”

He replied:
“There is no limit. It may be done.”

To run it five times, write:

I have a phrase: “Ask Heaven and Earth if They Are Well.”
Write it out — and so on.

To run it a thousand times, likewise write:

I have a phrase: “Ask Heaven and Earth if They Are Well.”
Write it out — and so on.
