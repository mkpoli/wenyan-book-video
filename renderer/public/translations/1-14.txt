As the sun and moon take turns in the sky.”

Someone asked:
“If I wish to run it five times,
may I?
If a thousand times,
may I?”

He replied:
“There is no limit that it cannot be done.”

To run it five times, write:

I have a phrase:
“Ask Heaven and Earth if They Are Well.”
Write it out
— and so on.

To run it a thousand times, likewise write:

I have a phrase:
“Ask Heaven and Earth if They Are Well.”
Write it out
— and so on.
