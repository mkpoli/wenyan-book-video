About the small numerals,
The Mak (漠) —,
is a trillionth (pico-, 10⁻¹²).
Ten Mak (one hundred billionth) are called Miewq (渺, 10⁻¹¹).
Ten Miewq (one ten-billionth) are called Qeoj (埃, 10⁻¹⁰).
Ten Qeoj (one billionth) are called Drin (塵, 10⁻⁹).
Ten Drin (one hundred millionth) are called Srae (沙, 10⁻⁸).
Ten Srae (one ten-millionth) are called Siem (纖, 10⁻⁷).
Ten Siem (one millionth) are called Muj (微, 10⁻⁶).
Ten Muj (one hundred thousandth) are called Hot (忽, 10⁻⁵).
Ten Hot (one ten-thousandth) are called Sy (絲, 10⁻⁴).
Ten Sy (one thousandth, milli-) are called Ghaw (毫, 10⁻³).
Ten Ghaw (one hundredth) are called Ly (釐, 10⁻²).
Ten Ly (one tenth) are called Pun (分, 10⁻¹).
Ten Pun (one) are called Qit (一, 10⁰).
