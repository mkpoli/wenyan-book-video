He answered:
“The joy of programming
lies in creation itself.
The Lady Nüwa first knew this joy;
Master Gongshu (Lu Ban) knew it again;
Master Ou Yeh-Tzu (Ou Yezi) knew it a third time.
Now this art exists as a pastime —
any common person may partake in it,
yet its delight remains the same.
How can one say there is no joy in it?”
